🍱 Food Donation Management System

Batch B1 | Group 05 | 23PCIT401L

Project Overview

This food donation system is designed to efficiently connect donors, one NGO, volunteers, and recipients through a centralized and automated platform. Donors can add details of leftover food such as quantity, type, and location, which becomes instantly visible in the system. Based on location, nearby volunteers are automatically notified, reducing delays and ensuring quick response.

Recipients can view available food and place requests in real time. The system then assigns the most suitable nearby volunteer, who collects the food from the donor and updates the status to "picked up," followed by delivery to the recipient. After successful delivery, the recipient confirms the receipt and provides a rating for the volunteer, ensuring transparency and accountability.

The admin, associated with a specific NGO, primarily monitors activities through a dashboard instead of manually controlling every step. This workflow ensures a fast, reliable, and scalable solution that minimizes food wastage while effectively delivering surplus food to those in need.
